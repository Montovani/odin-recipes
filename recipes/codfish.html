Codfishx

Description

A simple and flavorful codfish dish made with flaky white fish, sautéed vegetables, and olive oil. Light, healthy, and perfect for a quick dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 codfish fillets (fresh or soaked if salted)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic gloves
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tomato
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper for taste
  • Lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Pat the codfish dry and season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  4. Add bell pepper and tomato, cooking for 5–6 minutes.
  5. Move veggies to the side and add the codfish to the pan.
  6. Cook fish for 3–4 minutes per side until flaky and opaque.
  7. Spoon veggies over the fish and cook together for 2 more minutes.
  8. Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
