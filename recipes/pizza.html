Pizza

Description

A classic Italian Margherita pizza made with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. Crispy, light, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add slices of mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Remove from oven and add fresh basil leaves.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle salt.
  8. Slice and serve immediately.
